acrylic painting

Acrylic - is a great replacement for oil paints. Yes, oil is a classic. But we live in a hot, sunny country, and acrylic is more relevant here in my opinion - it does not fade, it is practically not inferior to oil in properties, if you need three-dimensional strokes with a palette knife, an excellent palette of colors, simplicity from a technological point of view and speed of work - it is worth noting separately .
I paint with acrylics mainly on Italian or Chinese canvases, on stretchers.
I have not developed a priority technique yet, it all depends on my mood, or on the mood and effects that I want to convey in a particular work.
